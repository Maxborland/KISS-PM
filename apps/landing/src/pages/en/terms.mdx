## Overview

These terms govern requests for access to, and participation in, the KISS PM closed alpha operated by the KISS PM team through `kisspm.app` ("KISS PM", "we", "us").

Submitting a request does not guarantee access and does not create an obligation for us to provide access. If a team receives a KISS PM workspace, additional terms for use, data processing, confidentiality, and liability may be agreed separately with an authorized representative of that team.

## Product status

KISS PM is in closed alpha. This means:

- the product is functionally limited and may change without backward compatibility;
- features, interfaces, scenarios, and integrations may be unavailable, changed, or disabled;
- bugs, downtime, test-data loss, and capability limits are possible during alpha;
- we do not guarantee 24/7 availability, SLA, preservation of all features, or fitness for a specific purpose.

The alpha is for scenario validation, feedback, and preparing the product for later stages. Do not use the alpha as the only source of critical operational data.

## Requesting access

By submitting a request, you confirm that:

1. you provide a valid work email and may use it for this communication;
2. you agree to receive a response from the KISS PM team about your request;
3. you have read the [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy/);
4. you understand that access is reviewed manually and may be declined without explanation.

A request may be declined because of alpha-wave limits, team profile, technical constraints, review capacity, security concerns, or other reasons.

## Access and account use

If access is approved, it is provided to a specific team or team representative. You may not transfer access to third parties, publish invitations, bypass access limits, or use an account outside the agreed purpose without written approval from KISS PM.

You are responsible for activity performed through access provided to you, unless a separate agreement says otherwise.

We may limit, suspend, or revoke access if:

- these terms are breached;
- access is used for unlawful, harmful, abusive, or excessively load-generating activity;
- there is a risk to product security, data, infrastructure, or other alpha participants;
- an alpha wave ends or participation conditions change.

## Fees

Participation in the closed alpha is free for approved teams unless separately agreed otherwise.

While the product is in alpha, you do not commit to paying for it later. Before commercial plans become available, we will share the terms and let participants continue under the new terms or end participation.

If you use the alpha with personal data governed by GDPR, UK GDPR, or similar data-protection laws, you are responsible for ensuring that you have a lawful basis to provide that data to KISS PM and that you do not upload data outside the agreed alpha scope.

Before processing personal data inside a workspace, the parties should agree the applicable controller/processor roles, processing instructions, categories of data, security measures, retention rules, and transfer safeguards in a separate data-processing or service agreement.

Until a separate agreement is signed, do not use the alpha to process production personal data, special-category data, or regulated confidential data.
