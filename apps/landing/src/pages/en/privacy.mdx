## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the KISS PM team operating `kisspm.app` ("KISS PM", "we", "us") processes personal data collected through the website, the closed alpha request form, email conversations about access, and related operational work around the KISS PM closed alpha.

KISS PM is in development. If a team receives access to a KISS PM workspace, processing inside that workspace may be governed by a separate agreement with the tenant administrator or another authorized representative of the team.

Contact for privacy requests: `hello@kisspm.app`.

## Controller and processor roles

For website visits, closed alpha requests, access conversations, consent records, and privacy requests, the KISS PM team operating `kisspm.app` acts as the controller of that data.

If an approved team receives access to a KISS PM workspace, the role split may be different. Depending on the agreed setup, your organization may act as the controller for workspace content, while KISS PM may act as a processor or service provider under a separate agreement. Do not use a workspace for production personal data until the applicable data-processing terms are agreed.

## Who this policy covers

We may process personal data about:

- website visitors;
- representatives of companies or teams who request access to the closed alpha;
- people who communicate with us about access, demos, feedback, or alpha participation.

We do not ask for special-category data, biometric data, payment data, government identifiers, or children's data. Please do not send those categories through the form or by email.

## Data we collect

When you request access to the closed alpha, you may provide:

- full name;
- work email;
- company;
- role or title;
- approximate number of active projects;
- optional context about what you want to test or where your current project-management work is manual.

When you use the website, we may process technical data needed to run the site, protect the form from spam, keep security logs, and record consent: request metadata, browser and device details, time of request, technical form-submission signals, IP-related security data, and consent status.

We do not use advertising pixels, behavioral retargeting, or third-party CDP tracking on the landing page. If we add analytics, cookies, or marketing tooling later, we will update this policy and request consent where required.

## Purposes and lawful bases

We process personal data for these purposes:

- to receive, review, and respond to closed alpha requests;
- to contact you about access, demos, or operational questions;
- to understand, in aggregate, which teams and roles need early access;
- to protect the website and form from spam, abuse, and technical failures;
- to keep internal records of consent and privacy requests;
- to comply with applicable legal obligations.

For visitors in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, our lawful bases may include consent, steps taken at your request before entering into an agreement, legitimate interests in operating and securing a B2B website, and legal obligations.

You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing `hello@kisspm.app`. Withdrawal does not affect processing that was lawful before we received the request.

## No sale and no unsolicited newsletters

We do not sell personal data. We do not use closed alpha requests for newsletters or mass marketing without a separate consent.

## Service providers and transfers

We may use providers for hosting, infrastructure, email delivery, website security, backup, and technical support. They receive only the data needed to perform their role and may not use it for their own purposes.

Some providers may process data outside your country. Where GDPR or UK GDPR applies and data is transferred outside the EEA or the UK, we will rely on an appropriate transfer mechanism, such as an adequacy decision, standard contractual clauses, or another lawful safeguard.

We may disclose data if required by law, by a competent authority, to protect our rights, or to prevent abuse or security incidents.

## Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above.

Indicative retention periods:

- closed alpha request: until the relevant alpha wave is selected or closed, or until consent is withdrawn;
- email conversation about access: up to 12 months after the last message, unless a longer period is needed to protect rights or comply with law;
- security and anti-spam logs: for the period needed to protect the website and investigate incidents;
- consent and privacy-request records: for the period needed to demonstrate compliance.

If Russian personal-data localization requirements apply to personal data of Russian citizens, primary recording, systematization, accumulation, storage, updating, and retrieval are performed using databases located in the Russian Federation.

## Security

We use organizational and technical measures intended to protect personal data against unauthorized or accidental access, alteration, disclosure, deletion, destruction, or other unlawful processing.

Access is limited to people who need it to review requests, contact users, support the website, secure the service, or comply with legal duties.

## Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

- access your personal data;
- correct inaccurate or incomplete data;
- request deletion of data;
- restrict or object to processing;
- receive a portable copy of data you provided;
- withdraw consent;
- complain to a data-protection authority.

To exercise these rights, email `hello@kisspm.app`. Include the email address used in the request and describe what you want us to do. We may ask for additional information if needed to confirm that the request relates to your data.

If data must be deleted but temporary retention is required by law or for protection of rights, we will restrict processing until deletion is possible.

## Automated decisions

The landing page does not make automated decisions that produce legal effects or similarly significant effects for a person. Closed alpha access is reviewed manually.

## Changes

We may update this policy when the website, request form, providers, legal requirements, or closed alpha process changes. The current version is published on this page, with the update date shown above.

## Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email `hello@kisspm.app`.
